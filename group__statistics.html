auto tl::DescriptiveStatistics< T >::coefficientOfVariation

Coefficient of Variation (CV) or Relative Standard Deviation (RSD) The coefficient of variation describes dispersion of data around the mean. It is the ratio of the standard deviation to the mean. The coefficient of variation is calculated as the standard deviation divided by the mean.

For a Population:

\[ C_V = \frac{\sigma}{\mu} \]

For a Sample:

\[ C_V = \frac{\sigma}{\bar{x}} \]

\[ \mu = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_i}{n} \]
